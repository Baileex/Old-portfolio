About Me

My journey into development

Hello, welcome to my portfolio, I'm Jess, a junior Full-Stack Developer looking to expand my knowledge and experience in the Tech sector.

The Beginning

My journey into the world of development is not the traditional journey, in fact it took a few years to get here. I have always had an interest in computers and technology from a young age, from joining computer clubs at school, to making websites with HTML & CSS as a hobby. However, at this age I was completely unaware of any careers relating to development (assuming all jobs in the sector were equivalent to the IT Crowd). Thus, I went on to complete a Biomedical Science degree at The University of Sheffield, however it was here that I further discovered my love of coding.

Combining Science and Code

During my final year at University, I wanted to try something new and different. I was presented the opportunity to learn R, a language for statistical computing and graphics, perfect for data science. Learning this language made me realise that coding is an endless learning opportunity, this inspired me to learn even more.

Becoming a Junior Developer

After my studies, I was determined to learn as much as I could about the industry, I attended a variety of different meetups and conferences throughout Greater Manchester and Manchester City Centre. The knowledge and advice I gained was invaluable and it ultimately lead me to Northcoders, were I graduated as a Full-Stack Developer after 12 weeks. After Northcoders I am familar with:

  • CSS/SCSS
  • Bootstrap
  • Javascript
